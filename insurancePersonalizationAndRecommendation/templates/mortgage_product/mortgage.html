The 5 characteristics of the “Handshake”-Loan

Easy

The “Handshake”-Loan is our standard product for residential loans between $10,000 and $50,000.

Convenient

You will receive the loan “in blank”: this means you won’t have to infer an additional property charge or get a notary to register the real estate. Thereby, you save a lot of time otherwise used for paper work, documents, certificates and running back and forth between the notary and other officials.

Inexpensive

For smaller loans the expenses for the registration of the parcel of land and the notary fees are disproportionate to your financial project.
With the “Handshake”-Loan you save these expenses.

Fast

You will receive the decision and the provision of your loan on the spot. Basically, you can directly take the money with you saving you about 4 weeks!

Familiar

Every customer we know and trust can receive the “Handshake”-Loan. Basic prerequisite is an existing checking account at “Sparkasse Mittelmosel” and existing residential property.

This type of combined financing consists of 3 phases:

Phase 1

You acquire a preliminary financing mortgage (e.g. 10 years fixed interest rate) with which you finance the acquisition/construction of your home. At the same time, you pay into a building savings contract of LBS. This contract gives you the right to obtain a future mortgage (say, in 10 years). Its interest rate will also already be fixed and guaranteed today. Due to the community of investors the interest rate will be very low.

Phase 2

After a certain time (e.g. 10 years) of accumulating savings for the building-savings-account the building-savings-loan is ready to be granted.
Its loan amount will be used to amortize the preliminary financing mortgage.

Phase 3

You amortize the new building-savings-loan month after month and pay a guaranteed fixed interest rate until complete amortization.
In Germany, you can additionally benefit from government support (“Riester-Förderung”).

Possible motives for the use of an LBS Building-Savings-loan

  • Fixed interest rate for the entire loan duration
  • Affordable rates
  • Government support
